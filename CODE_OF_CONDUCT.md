# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect all contributors and maintainers to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical facts and outcomes.
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification before escalation.
- Avoid harassment, insults, discrimination, and disruptive behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or derogatory language.
- Public or private harassment.
- Doxxing or publication of private information.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after moderation feedback.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- Repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Any official project communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation for violations.
Serious or repeated violations can result in permanent bans.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to project maintainers through official HauntedMC support channels:

- https://www.hauntedmc.nl/support
